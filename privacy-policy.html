Privacy
Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Information We Collect

When you submit a quote request through our website, we collect the following personal information:

  • Your name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Suburb or location
  • Photos of the damage you submit via our quote form

We do not require you to create an account. All information is submitted voluntarily when you choose to contact us for a quote.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely for the purpose of running our business:

  • To assess your repair job and provide you with an accurate quote
  • To communicate with you about your enquiry and any booked repair work
  • To follow up on enquiries if we have not heard back from you
  • To issue invoices and maintain business records

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your details are used only in the course of delivering our repair service to you.

Photos & Images

Photos you submit through our quote form are used solely to assess the damage and provide you with an accurate quote for repair work.

We may wish to use anonymised before-and-after photos of completed jobs for marketing purposes (website, social media). We will only do this with your explicit permission. We will never publish a photo that identifies your home or property without your consent.

Cookies

Our website uses a minimal number of cookies to support basic site functionality. Specifically, we store your theme preference (dark or light mode) in your browser's local storage so the site remembers your choice between visits.

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party analytics cookies that monitor your browsing behaviour across other websites.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses a small number of third-party services to function:

  • Formspree — we use Formspree to receive quote form submissions. When you submit a form, your data passes through Formspree's servers and is delivered to us by email. Formspree's privacy policy is available at formspree.io.
  • Google Fonts — we load typefaces from Google Fonts. This may result in your browser making a request to Google's servers. Google's privacy policy applies to this interaction.

We do not use any other third-party data processors, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

Data Retention

Enquiry and customer data is held for up to 2 years from the date of initial contact, for the purpose of maintaining business records and following up on past jobs if required.

You can request that we delete your personal information at any time by contacting us at the email address below. We will action deletion requests promptly.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about how we handle your personal information, or to request access to or deletion of your data, please contact us:
